# How to Cite and Reuse This Work

### Stewardship, Provenance, and Ethical Reuse

#### Purpose of This Page

This page provides guidance for citing, reusing, and extending the _Humanity++ / Transformative Kindness GPT_ materials in ways that preserve **authorship clarity, ethical intent, and educational integrity**.

The goal is not restriction, but **responsible stewardship** in AI-mediated knowledge production.

***

### Recommended Citation

When citing this work in academic, educational, or professional contexts, please use the following format (adapt as needed for your citation style):

> Doore, K. _Humanity++: Vital Intelligence Model and Transformative Kindness GPT_. GitBook.\
> Licensed under CC BY-NC-SA 4.0.

Where possible, include:

* a direct link to the GitBook
* the specific section or page referenced
* the version date (GitBook history provides this)

***

### Meaningful Human Control and Authorship

This work is developed under the principle of **Meaningful Human Control**.

AI systems may be used in the creation, refinement, or extension of this material, but:

* conceptual intent
* interpretive judgment
* ethical framing

remain human responsibilities.

When reusing or extending this work, contributors are encouraged to:

* document how AI tools were used
* describe where human judgment intervened
* reflect on limitations or uncertainties introduced

This transparency supports shared learning rather than authority claims.

### Appropriate and Inappropriate Uses

#### Appropriate Uses

* curriculum design
* studio-based learning
* research and theory development
* AI literacy programs
* institutional reflection and reform discussions

#### Inappropriate Uses

* commercial exploitation without consent
* persuasive or manipulative social engineering
* removal of attribution
* use in systems that displace human discernment

These boundaries are aligned with the **Kindness Attractor** and TAI-KPI principles.

***

### Responsibility of Reusers

Reuse implies **interpretive responsibility**.

Those who adapt or apply this work are responsible for:

* contextual appropriateness
* potential harms or misreadings
* maintaining clarity about AI’s non-agency

This work does not confer authority; it invites care.

***

### Closing Note

The value of this work lies not in ownership, but in **how it is carried forward**.

Careful citation, transparent reuse, and reflective adaptation help ensure that _Transformative Kindness_ remains a resource for learning rather than a tool for capture.
